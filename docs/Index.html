Core modules

This view is designed to help a SOC analyst understand what happened, what matters, and what to do next.

Alert Triage

Prioritizes alerts by context, severity, confidence, and likely business impact.

Threat Intel Enrichment

Looks up IPs, domains, hashes, and indicators to add external context to each alert.

Event Correlation

Connects related signals into a single investigation case instead of isolated events.

Analyst Summary

Generates a compact summary of observations, hypotheses, and next steps.

Operational scenario

Example portfolio narrative showing how the SOC workflow supports investigation speed and decision making.

Incoming Alert

Suspicious authentication activity is detected from a new IP and is sent to the triage pipeline.

Enrichment + Correlation

The system adds threat intel and checks whether the activity matches other endpoint or network signals.

Analyst Decision

The analyst reviews the summary, validates the evidence, and decides whether to escalate or close.
